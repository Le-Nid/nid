# Unified Inbox

If you've connected multiple Gmail accounts, the **unified inbox** lets you view all your emails in a single view.

---

## Overview

The **Unified Inbox** page merges emails from all your connected Gmail accounts into a **single timeline**, sorted by date.

Each email is identified by a **colored tag** indicating the Gmail account it belongs to.

> 📸 *Suggested screenshot: Unified Inbox page with emails from different accounts, each identified by a different colored tag*

---

## How It Works

1. The application queries **all your Gmail accounts simultaneously**
2. Results are merged and sorted by date in descending order
3. If an account is temporarily unavailable, emails from the other accounts are still displayed

---

## Usage

- The search bar works across **all accounts** simultaneously
- Click on an email to open it in the built-in reader
- The colored tag tells you which account each email belongs to

---

## Limitations

- The unified inbox is **read-only**: bulk actions are not available from this view
- To act on emails (delete, archive, etc.), navigate to **My Emails** and select the relevant account
